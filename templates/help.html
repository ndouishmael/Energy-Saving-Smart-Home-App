Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does the app save energy in smart homes?

A: The app automatically disables electric appliances when they are not in use and switches off lights when the room is empty.

Q: Can I customize the app's settings for my specific needs?

A: Yes, the app provides customizable settings to adjust energy-saving preferences according to your requirements.

Q: How do I get started with the app?

A: To get started, download and install the app on your smart home device. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up the app and configure your preferences.
